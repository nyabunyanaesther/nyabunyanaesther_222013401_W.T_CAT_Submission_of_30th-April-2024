About Student Voting System

Students are allowed to insert in candidates and view the position that needs the candidate so he/she can participate.

The Student Voting System is designed to facilitate the voting process in educational institutions. It allows students to participate in various elections, including student council elections, club elections, and other institutional decisions.

This system provides an easy-to-use interface for students to cast their votes securely and efficiently. It ensures transparency and fairness in the electoral process by maintaining accurate records of votes cast and verifying voter eligibility.

Administration

Admin is allowed to insert the position and view the candidate and the votes.

With the Student Voting System, administrators can manage candidate nominations, set election rules, and monitor voting activities in real-time. It streamlines the entire voting process, from voter registration to result announcement, making it a valuable tool for promoting democratic participation within the student community.
